Top Three Spots

My favourite spots for others to discover on campus are:

Favourite spot to grab a refresher

My #1 favourite spot to grab a cold drink, a refresher and some snacks is the Starbucks located in the UCC (University Community Centre). I think the best time to go to the Starbucks is after classes with friends. but its usually much more busy at this time.

I like going here for refreshers because Starbucks offers a huge variety of different drinks.

There are two Starbucks in the UCC, but I prefer the one on the main floor because one of the employees remember my order!

Favourite spot to study

The best spot to study would definitely be the Natural Science Building. The building is super bright which helps with staying awake, and if you have a class in the building, you can come early and study while waiting

There's also a nice cafe to grab a quick drink and vendine machines with a bunch of great snacks!

The building is right in the middle of campus which is always a plus.

Favourite spot to have dinner

My favourite spot to grab food would for sure be The Spoke, also located in the UCC! There's lots of great options, but my go to order is their fries with chicken fingers.

Going to The Spoke with friends for dinner is always a fun experience, and lots of people say they have the best bagels.

There's also a breakfast bar with smoothies, and the music played in The Spoke is always good!
